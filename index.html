What We Do

TheCoffeePlaces is a unified platform for cafés and restaurants to engage directly with customers. Our solution enables:

  • Cost-effective, digital loyalty programs (no expensive custom builds)
  • Curated events and targeted deals to build vibrant communities
  • AI-powered personalization that tailors every dining experience

How It Works

  • Check-In: Customers check in via our app, alerting the café instantly.
  • Loyalty: Digital stamp cards update automatically.
  • AI Insights: Baristas access real-time customer preferences.
  • Personalized Upselling: Tailored recommendations boost sales.
  • Event Management: Schedule and promote exclusive events and deals.
  • Feedback Loop: In-app feedback refines future experiences.

Why Choose Us

  • All-in-one platform for loyalty, events, and AI personalization
  • Empowers businesses to engage directly with customers
  • Builds a thriving multi-sided network for greater visibility and customer loyalty
